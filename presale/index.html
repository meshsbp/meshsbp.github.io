Mesh

Mesh is a token on the Vite network. It uses a Snapshot Block Producer (SBP) to reward its holders. All funds gained in the presale go to listing Mesh and funding an SBP. Unlike other SBPs on the Vite network, MeshSBP will be the only SBP to distribute 100% of its rewards.
