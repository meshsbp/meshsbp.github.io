What is Mesh?

Mesh is a token on the Vite network. It uses the daily rewards earned by a Snapshot Blcok Producer (SBP) to reward its holders. All funds received in the presale will go towards listing Mesh and funding an SBP. Unlike other SBPs in the Vite network, MeshSBP will be the only SBP to distribute 100% of its rewards.

Features

Mesh & MeshSBP

Mesh distributes 100% of its daily rewards as a Snapshot Block Producer to Mesh holders, It guarantees a much higher ROI compared to other SBPs.

Interface

Mesh is committed to accessibilty. There will be a clean interface to inteeract with your Mesh tokens. This way, you will always know whay to do, and how to do it.

Trustless & Decentralized

Mesh runs on decentralized and trustless smart contracts, giving you peace of mind when it comes to your investment.

ViteX

Mesh will be listed on ViteX, a decentralized exchange built into the vite network. Mesh's listing price will be at the ViteX price floor (0.00000001 Vite). This guarantees that that the value will never fall below what you pay during the presale.

Communications

Mesh promises to maintain open and transparent communications with its investors.
